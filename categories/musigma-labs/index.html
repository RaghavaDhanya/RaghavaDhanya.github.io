Showcase: BPMN Pipeline Platform

Business Process Model and Notation (BPMN) is a graphical notation standard for business processes. Think of it as a flowchart for business processes. It is widely used in the industry for modeling business processes. Now, why would you use BPMN for automating / pipelining? We don’t use UML for writing software right? Yes, but BPMN’s adoption for automation and pipelining stemmed from its ability to cater to both technical and non-technical users. Whether orchestrating simple data flows or complex machine learning pipelines, BPMN empowered users to architect automated workflows with ease and flexibility.
